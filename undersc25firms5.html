Competition / Undercutting Rivals under Switching Costs / 1 to 25 Competitors

Purpose:  To calculate the profit-maximizing price that a new firm (your firm) should set in order to grab consumers from competing firms. This model assumes that competing firms hold their prices constant, and that consumers bear switching costs when switching brands. The user (you) inputs prices and market shares of your rivals, as well as marginal cost, and swtiching costs, and How-to-Price computes your profit maximizing price.
Remark: Here, competitors are not allowed to adjust their prices; see our next page for a calculation of an equilibrium when all firms adjust their prices in order to preserve or protect their market shares.
