Competition / Homogeneous Products

[Class of linear demand functions]

Purpose: (1) To calculate the profit-maximizing quantity produced (best-response) by a firm selling in a market with a known number of rival firms, who produce (or maintain inventories) at a given (not adjustable) known aggregate output level. (2) To calculate the market (Cournot) equilibrium assuming that the competiting firms are also able to adjust their output levels in response to others'.
