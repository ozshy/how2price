Competition / Switching Costs / Equilibrium Prices / 2 to 9 Sellers

Purpose: To calculate the equilibrium prices under which each firm continues to maintian its existing market share. The model assume that consumers bear switching costs of switching brands. The user (you) inputs market shares, marginal cost, and swtiching costs, and How-to-Price computes equilibrium prices (the so-called undercut-proof equilibrium).
