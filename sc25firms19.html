Competition / Switching Costs / 2 to 25 Sellers

Purpose: To calculate the consumer switching costs of a consumer who purchases the competing brand instead of the brand he purchased earlier. The user (you) input prices and market shares, and How-to-Price computes how much it costs for a consumer to switch to a competiting brand.

Inputting demand and technology data:
Please, always click  before changing or submitting the following parameters:
  • Enter the number of sellers (firms) N in this market (2-25): 
  • The marginal production cost (c). Enter c in this box (c > 0 or c = 0):
  • Below, enter the price charged by each seller (pi > 0), and the seller's market share (0<mi<100)
    (Remark: if, for example, you entered N = 6 (default), the program will ignore all prices and market shares of sellers 7, 8, and 9, and so on...)
