Single Seller / Tying / 2 Goods / 2 to 25 Consumer Types

Purpose: To compute the single-seller's profit-maximizing strategy regarding tying of products and selling them as packages. The user (you) input willingness to pay for each good by each consumer type, as well as the number of consumers of each type, and the seller's cost parameters.
Then, How-to-PriceTM computes whether tying is profitable, and how to price the packages as well as each product sold separately.
